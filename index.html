ABOUT ME

Born and raised in France.I learnt how to make pizza at the age of five. With my grandfather as a mentor, he showd me all the tricks and techniques on how to make a pizza. It stared out as a hobby just to pass time as I wan't that into sports at the time and I needed to pass time with something productive.

Right now I'm glad I decided to focus on it as a passion of mine and look at where it has gotten me now. We all have our inner passion and mine started making me money when I was fifteen. I used that to go to culinary school to learn from the greats and see hoe they approach there cooks.

Find your passion and hold on to it. Don't let anyone tell you otherwise.

WHERE IT ALL STARTED


On the outskirts of bordeaux. I started out with a small popup tent where I sold pizza rolls an pepporoni pizza. I sat there for ten saturdays before I got any customers that weren't people who just came for the tasting experience. Luckily I wasn't paying for the space aroung since the owner was a close friend to my mother and worked in Paris.

I won't lie to you and say some magic-man came up to me with interest of caling my business. It pure sweat and tears that got me here. My parents were big fans so as they went to work they would carry the pizza rolls I would make and share amongst there colleagues.

Interest grew and somome of there colleagues would show up on the saturdays to know the chef behind the cook. Slowly the number increased to the point that I had to stay up all night to make enough dough for the saturday customers. Sooon I had to maove to larger spaces to accomodate the customers and fund myself in a space in the main city.

As they say, the rest was history
